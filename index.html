About Us

Our Logo

The SabaiCraft Treasures logo encapsulates the platform's essence. Sabai grass craft is a traditional handicraft that has been practiced for centuries in various regions of India. The craft involves weaving intricate designs and patterns using the fibers of the Sabai grass plant. To represent this unique art form, this logo is is made which feature an Sabai grass plant itself, and stylised representation of the weaving process.

The dark olive color signifies the natural origins of sabai grass, while the woven palm/hand structure signifies the manual craftsmanship involved in creating these crafts. This interwoven design embodies the meticulous crafting process and underscores the platform's commitment to safeguarding and celebrating this tradition.



Our Mascot

The mascot made for the sabai grass craft is a beautiful representation of the art form and the culture associated with it. The belly of the mascot has been designed to represent the basket made out of sabai grass, which is one of the primary items created using this craft. The mascot is depicted as a woman, paying homage to the fact that most of the artisans who practice this craft are women. The leaves on the crown or headgear represent the sabai grass itself, which is the main raw material used in the creation of the craft.

The design of the mascot is a celebration of the unique artistry and traditional knowledge that is passed down through generations of skilled artisans and serves as a reminder of the importance of preserving and promoting this valuable cultural heritage.
